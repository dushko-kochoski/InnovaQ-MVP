Frequently asked

Do I need a developer to use InnovaQ?

No. You pick a template — for example "invoice payment reminder" — set your own rule, and switch it on. If your firm can use e-banking, it can use InnovaQ.

Can it connect to the software I already use?

On Бизнис and Про, workflows can receive webhooks from and send requests to any system with an internet connection — your invoicing program, web shop, or CRM.

Is there a contract lock-in?

No. Plans are month-to-month. Cancel from the dashboard and you won't be billed again.

Do you issue a proper invoice?

Yes — a regular VAT invoice from InnovaQ DOOEL Skopje for every payment, delivered to your email automatically. (Of course it's automated.)

Try it on your most annoying task first.

Most customers start with one workflow — usually the invoice reminders — and add more once they see Friday afternoons come back.
