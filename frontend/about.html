Why InnovaQ exists

Walk into any accounting office in Centar, any wholesale depot near the ring road, any dental clinic in Aerodrom — and you'll find the same thing: capable people doing the same five manual tasks every single day. Calling about unpaid invoices. Texting appointment reminders one by one. Checking a notebook to see whose contract expires this month.

The big automation platforms weren't built for them. They're priced in dollars, documented in jargon, and assume you have an IT person. Most Macedonian SMEs have an "IT person" only in the sense that someone's nephew once fixed the printer.

So we built InnovaQ: one simple automation engine, with ready-made workflow templates for the five industries we know best — accounting, wholesale trade, real estate, logistics, and healthcare. Priced in denars. Supported in Macedonian. Designed so the owner can set it up personally, on a Tuesday, between two client meetings.
