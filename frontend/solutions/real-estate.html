2. Permit & paperwork deadline tracking

Before

Building permits, cadastre extracts, energy certificates — each with its own expiry, tracked in an Excel sheet someone updates "when they remember". An expired document surfaces mid-sale and freezes the deal for weeks.

With InnovaQ

Every document's expiry date is watched daily. Two weeks before anything lapses, the responsible agent gets an email naming the property, the document, and the office to renew it at. Deals stop dying of paperwork.

3. Rental contract renewals

Before

You manage thirty rentals across Centar and Debar Maalo. A lease quietly expires, the tenant stays "informally", and the landlord finds out their contract lapsed two months ago — and asks why they pay you a fee at all.

With InnovaQ

Thirty days before any lease ends, the landlord gets a Viber message proposing renewal terms, and your agent gets the task in their inbox. Renewals happen on time, in writing — and your fee justifies itself.
