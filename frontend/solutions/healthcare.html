Template: Appointment reminder via Viber

1. Viber appointment reminders

Before

Your receptionist spends the last hour of every shift calling tomorrow's patients one by one. Half don't answer; a few were booked three weeks ago and honestly forgot. Tomorrow, two chairs sit empty anyway.

With InnovaQ

Every patient automatically gets a Viber message 24 hours before their appointment — date, time, doctor, and a way to confirm or cancel. Reminders go out while your receptionist greets the patients who actually showed up.

Template: No-show follow-up

2. No-show recovery

Before

A patient misses their cleaning, nobody follows up, and they quietly drift away — until they reappear two years later with a problem that ten check-ups would have caught. The no-show cost you the slot and the patient.

With InnovaQ

When an appointment is marked as missed, the patient automatically receives a friendly Viber message the same day with a link to rebook. Most reschedule within 48 hours — no awkward calls, no lost patients.

Template: New patient intake

3. New patient intake

Before

New patients fill a paper form on a clipboard in the waiting room — allergies, history, contact details — and the receptionist retypes it into the computer between phone calls. Typos in phone numbers mean reminders that never arrive.

With InnovaQ

Patients complete the intake form online before they arrive. The moment it's submitted, InnovaQ creates the record in your clinic system — correctly spelled, instantly available, and already wired into the reminder workflows above.

Full chairs. Found patients. A front desk that breathes.
