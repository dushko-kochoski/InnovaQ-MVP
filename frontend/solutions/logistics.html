Three workflows logistics firms switch on first

Template: Shipment status notification

1. Shipment status updates to customers

Before

A truck waits four hours at the Tabanovce border crossing, and your dispatcher spends those four hours on the phone repeating "it's at the border" to every customer with goods on board.

With InnovaQ

Every status change — loaded, at border, customs cleared, out for delivery — automatically fires a Viber message to the affected customers. The phone goes quiet; the dispatcher dispatches.

Template: Delivery confirmation

2. Instant delivery confirmations

Before

Proof of delivery is a signed paper in the driver's cab — until Thursday, when he's back in Skopje. Customers wait days for confirmation, and billing waits with them. Sometimes the paper never makes it at all.

With InnovaQ

The moment a delivery is marked complete, the customer gets an email with confirmation and the proof-of-delivery link — and your billing can invoice the same day instead of next week.

Template: Invoice reconciliation

3. Carrier invoice reconciliation

Before

At month-end someone compares carrier and partner invoices against shipment records, line by line, in two Excel windows. A 4.000 МКД overcharge hides in row 187 — and usually gets paid without anyone noticing.

With InnovaQ

InnovaQ checks invoices against your shipment records every day and emails you only the mismatches — shipment, expected amount, billed amount. You dispute discrepancies while they're fresh, not in the next quarter.

Fewer phone calls. Faster invoicing. No silent overcharges.

All three workflows are ready-made templates — connect your tracking system and switch them on.
