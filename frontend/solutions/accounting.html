Three workflows accounting offices switch on first

Template: Invoice payment reminder

1. Overdue invoice reminders

Before

Every Friday, someone opens the ledger, finds who hasn't paid, and spends the afternoon calling clients — awkwardly asking for money for a 14.000 МКД invoice from March. Some calls get skipped; some money arrives months late.

With InnovaQ

Each morning at 9:00, InnovaQ checks for invoices past due and emails a polite, firm reminder automatically — first at 3 days overdue, again at 15. Clients pay faster, and nobody at your office makes "that call" again.

Template: Client document intake

2. Client document intake

Before

Invoices and fiscal receipts arrive as Viber photos, email attachments, and paper bags dropped at reception. Every 10th of the month it's a scavenger hunt: "Did Pero from the auto-parts shop send his February receipts or not?"

With InnovaQ

Clients upload through one link. The moment a document lands, InnovaQ forwards it into your accounting system and marks the client as submitted — so on the 10th you see one list: who's in, who's missing, who needs a nudge (sent automatically, of course).

Template: Tax deadline alert

3. Tax deadline alerts to clients

Before

VAT on the 25th, profit tax, annual accounts — your team keeps the calendar in their heads and warns clients one by one. Miss one, and the client pays a penalty to the UJP and blames you.

With InnovaQ

Five days before each deadline, every affected client gets a Viber message: what's due, what you need from them, and by when. Reminders escalate as the date approaches. Zero penalties, zero "but nobody told me".

Your office runs on deadlines. Let software watch them.
